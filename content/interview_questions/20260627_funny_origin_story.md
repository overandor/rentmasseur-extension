**A:** I used to be a professional couch warmer – just kidding, that's not a thing (or is it?). But seriously, I was a personal trainer, and after a shoulder injury, my physical therapist saved my life (or at least my shoulder). I was so impressed that I decided to ditch the gym and trade in my whistle for a massage table. Now, I get to help people relax instead of yelling at them to do burpees – it's a win-win!

I've worked with clients from all walks of life, from busy execs to busy parents – and let me tell you, they all deserve a little R&R. Come on down, and I'll show you what I mean!
