But seriously, communicate with me about any areas of tension or discomfort, and we'll work together to make sure you get the most out of your massage. Trust me, your body (and I) will thank you.

## Q4: What's the biggest misconception people have about getting a massage, and how do you dispel it?
**A:** I think people often think that massages are just for luxury or pampering, but really, they're an essential part of self-care. I've had clients come in with chronic pain or stress, and after a massage, they're like a new person. So, if you're thinking, "I don't deserve a massage," let me tell you, you absolutely do.
