#### Deep Tissue: The Knot-Killing Ninja
Deep Tissue massage is like the Special Forces of the massage world. It's for those who need a little (okay, a lot) of extra help in releasing the tension that's been building up in their muscles. So, which one is right for you? Well, that depends on what you're looking for.
