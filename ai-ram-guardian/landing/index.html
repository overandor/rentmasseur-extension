Award-grade mechanics, not decoration

Glass outside. Discipline inside.

The UI is calm because the engine is careful. The app avoids theatrical cleanup and only acts when it can explain what happened.

FLOW

The machine follows your attention. Non-focused AI helper processes ease down; focused helpers restore. Event-driven from macOS focus changes, not polling theater.

SOOTHE

When thermal state is serious, Guardian can demote the hottest eligible helper and re-measure later. Thermal receipts are labeled observational.

Kalman pressure

Memory pressure forecast with visible uncertainty. Warnings only fire when the trend clears its own error bar.

Anomaly learning

Per-app RSS baselines are learned on-device. The app compares each AI tool against its own normal, not a fake universal threshold.

Whisper HUD

A tiny glass receipt appears under the menu bar when an action lands, then disappears. The guardian is visible only when it matters.

Self-audit

The app publishes its own RAM and CPU cost in the UI. Efficiency claims are measured beside the workload they supervise.
