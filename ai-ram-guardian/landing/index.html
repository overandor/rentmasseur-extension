Built for the agent desktop.

AI coding tools changed the Mac workload: helper processes, language servers, local agents, indexers, renderers, and thermal spikes. Guardian treats the machine as a measured system, not a trash can to empty.

FLOW

The machine follows your attention. Non-focused AI helpers ease down; focused helpers restore. Event-driven from macOS focus changes, not polling theater.

SOOTHE

When thermal state is serious, Guardian can demote the hottest eligible helper and re-measure later. Thermal receipts are labeled observational.

Kalman pressure

Memory pressure forecast with visible uncertainty. Warnings fire only when the trend clears its own error bar.

Anomaly learning

Per-app RSS baselines are learned on-device. Each AI tool is compared against its own normal, not a fake universal threshold.

Whisper HUD

A tiny glass receipt appears under the menu bar when an action lands, then disappears. The guardian is visible only when it matters.

Self-audit

The app publishes its own RAM and CPU cost in the UI. Efficiency claims are measured beside the workload they supervise.
